Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Flying With Joel uses cookies and browser storage technologies for the following purposes:

2.1 Essential/Functional Cookies

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type

preferredTimezone Remembers your selected timezone for stream times Until deleted localStorage

2.2 Third-Party Cookies

3. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our Website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You have the following options:

  • Accept All: All cookies and tracking technologies are enabled
  • Reject: Only essential functional cookies are used; third-party and analytics cookies are disabled

You can change your consent preference at any time by revisiting the consent banner.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Refuse to accept cookies
  • Alert you when a cookie is being set
  • Delete cookies from your device

Browser-Specific Instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

5. localStorage vs Cookies

We primarily use browser localStorage rather than traditional cookies. localStorage:

  • Stores data locally on your device
  • Does NOT send data to our servers automatically
  • Persists until you manually clear it or withdraw consent
  • Is NOT sent with HTTP requests (unlike cookies)
  • Offers greater privacy than traditional cookies

6. Analytics and Tracking

No Analytics Platforms:

We currently do NOT use any analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Matomo) that track individual user behavior. This means we do not track page views, user journeys, or demographic information. We respect your privacy by default.

7. GDPR Compliance

Our cookie usage complies with GDPR requirements:

  • We obtain explicit consent before non-essential cookies are set
  • Consent is opt-in (not pre-checked)
  • You can easily withdraw or modify consent
  • We provide clear disclosure of third-party services

8. Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some Website features may not work as intended:

  • Your timezone preference will not be remembered
  • You may see the cookie consent banner repeatedly

9. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
