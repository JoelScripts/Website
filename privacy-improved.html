Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Flying With Joel ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the website https://flyingwithjoel.co.uk/ (the "Website"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Website.

We're committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Form Submissions: Name, email address, and message content
  • Stream Suggestions: Information submitted through our suggestions page

Information Collected Automatically

  • Timezone Preferences: Stored locally on your device using localStorage (only with consent)
  • Cookie Consent: Your privacy choices stored on your device

Third-Party Services

These services may collect data when you use them:

  • Google Fonts: Limited font delivery data
  • Twitch: May collect viewing analytics (see Twitch Privacy Policy)
  • LinkTree: May collect traffic data (see LinkTree Privacy Policy)

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your data based on:

✓ Your Consent

For timezone preferences and optional cookies

✓ Contract Performance

When necessary to provide website functionality

✓ Legitimate Interests

To improve website functionality and user experience

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests
  • Remember your timezone preference for stream scheduling
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage (On Your Device)

Timezone preferences and consent choices are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is only accessible by this website
  • Can be deleted anytime by clearing browser data

Contact Submissions (On Our Servers)

Email messages are transmitted securely via HTTPS and stored on our hosting provider's secure servers with appropriate security measures.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all data we hold about you

Right to Correction

Request corrections to inaccurate data

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data

Right to Portability

Request your data in a portable format

Withdraw Consent

Change your cookie preferences anytime

Right to Object

Object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@flyingwithjoel.co.uk

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and localStorage to:

  • Store your cookie consent preferences
  • Remember your selected timezone
No Tracking Analytics:

We do NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics/tracking services. We respect your privacy by default.

For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

8. Data Retention

  • Local Storage Data: Retained until you clear your browser data or withdraw consent
  • Contact Form Data: Retained for 2 years or as required by law
  • Consent Records: Retained for 3 years for legal compliance

9. Your Data Protection Rights

If you're not satisfied with our response to your data rights requests, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., ICO in the UK).
